Our Projects

Autonomous Surveillance Drone 2024

An autonomous aerial platform designed for surveillance and terrain analysis, integrating real-time vision, navigation intelligence, and adaptive control systems for safe and efficient flight operations.

This project focuses on building a fully autonomous surveillance drone capable of operating in complex and dynamic environments. The system combines computer vision–based obstacle detection, GPS-assisted navigation, and real-time sensor fusion to ensure stable and intelligent flight. Applications include disaster monitoring, security patrols, and large-area mapping, emphasizing robustness, scalability, and real-world deployment readiness.

Python • OpenCV • ROS • PX4
Team: A. Kumar, S. Verma
