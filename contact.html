Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join?

Apply during our recruitment drive at the start of each semester.

What skills are needed?

No prior skills required! We provide workshops on coding and hardware.

Are there membership fees?

No, membership to the Robotics Club is completely free for NIT Patna students.

Can I use the lab?

Yes, active members have access to the lab equipment for approved projects.

Do you participate in competitions?

Yes, we represent NITP in national events like Robocon and various tech-fests.
