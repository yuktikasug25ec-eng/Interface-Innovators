Our History

The Robotics Club at NIT Patna embodies the spirit of innovation and technical excellence.

Our Beginning (2015–16)

Founded by Nripendra Saraj Sir at NIT Patna, the Robotics Club was created as a hub for students passionate about innovation and technology.

Diverse Expertise

Our members range from seasoned coders and brilliant designers to hardware scientists, creating a dynamic environment for collaboration and learning.

Competitions & Achievements

Members have proudly represented NIT Patna at events like the Smart India Hackathon, currently led by our Captain Dheeraj Kumar Sir.

Skill Enhancement & Awareness

The club focuses on enhancing technical skills while exposing students to the fascinating world of robotics, automation, and electronics.

Events & Festivities

With a rich roster of engaging and exciting events, this year’s techno-cultural fest provided an exhilarating experience for all participants.

Mission

The Robotics Club of NIT Patna promotes hands-on learning in robotics, fostering technical skills, innovation, and teamwork while connecting students with real-world and industry-relevant challenges.

Vision

The Robotics Club of NIT Patna envisions shaping the future by leading technological innovation, building intelligent machines, and inspiring students to explore AI, automation, and advanced robotics while nurturing the next generation of creative and capable engineering leaders.

Objectives

The Robotics Club of NIT Patna promotes hands-on learning through workshops and projects in robotics, coding, and system design. It nurtures innovation by turning ideas into real robots, builds industry-relevant technical skills, and fosters collaboration while exploring future and real-world applications of robotics.

Club Highlights

Competitions

NIT Patna’s Robotics Club hosts engaging tech events like Path Enigma, a maze-solving robot competition, and participates in national platforms such as NRL and Robofest 5.0, fostering hands-on learning and skills across mechanical, electrical, and programming domains.

Workshops

The Robotics Club at NIT Patna conducts hands-on workshops on Arduino, robot design, programming, and IoT, helping beginners and tech enthusiasts build practical skills, foster innovation, and prepare for future tech challenges.

Research

The Robotics Club at NIT Patna drives hands-on innovation across AI/ML, IoT, 5G, cybersecurity, and 3D printing, building intelligent robots to solve real-world problems while fostering collaboration, future-ready skills, and employability.

Ongoing Activities

The club actively conducts year-round events, workshops (such as on Arduino and ESP32), and projects on topics including IoT, drones, 3D designing, and AI-based systems.

Event Highlights

Major internal events like "Machine Mayhem", "Robowars", and "Tiger's Nest" draw significant participation and audience engagement.

Competition Representation

Members have consistently represented NIT Patna in various inter-college technical competitions, ideathons, and hackathons, including the Smart India Hackathon.

Idea Festival 2025

A club member secured 2nd prize among over 25,000 teams at the Idea Festival 2025, organized by Startup Bihar, for an innovative VTOL-UAV (Vertical Take-off and Landing Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) idea.

ISRO Robotics Challenge 2025

The club engineered an "Autonomous Navigation Aerial Vehicle" (ANAV) with a custom flight controller for the "Fly Me on Mars" theme, demonstrating advanced capabilities in autonomous navigation for extraterrestrial exploration.

Community Outreach

The club actively promotes robotics among students in nearby schools and colleges, providing resources and opportunities for broader learning.
